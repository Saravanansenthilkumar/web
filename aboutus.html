About Us

Welcome to PT Om Muruga Silks, where tradition meets elegance in the world of sarees and garments. For the past 17 years, we have been dedicated to bringing you the finest sarees and garments, blending timeless craftsmanship with contemporary designs. Established in 2007, our journey began with a passion for creating exquisite sarees and garments that celebrate the rich heritage of textile artistry. Our commitment to excellence is reflected in every saree and garment we produce. We source the finest materials and work with skilled artisans who pour their expertise and creativity into each piece. Our collection includes a wide range of sarees, from traditional weaves to modern interpretations, as well as a variety of garments that cater to diverse tastes and occasions. At PT Om Muruga Silks, we believe in sustainability and ethical practices. We are dedicated to using eco-friendly materials and processes to minimize our environmental impact. Our commitment to fair trade ensures that every artisan involved in our production is treated with respect and fairness. Our mission is to continue innovating while staying true to our roots. We strive to exceed our customers' expectations by offering unique, high-quality sarees and garments, along with exceptional customer service. Whether you are looking for a saree for a special occasion or everyday wear, we are here to provide you with the best options. Thank you for being a part of our journey. We look forward to many more years of celebrating the beauty and elegance of sarees and garments with you.
